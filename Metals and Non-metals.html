📝 Assertion & Reason Questions (1 Mark)

A1. (31/2/1)

Assertion (A): The extraction of metals from their sulphide ores cannot take place without roasting.
Reason (R): Roasting converts sulphide ores directly into metals.

A2. (31/2/2)

Assertion (A): Metals in the middle of activity series are found as sulphides or carbonates.
Reason (R): Sulphide ores are calcinated and carbonate ores are roasted to extract metals.

A3. (31/5/1)

Assertion (A): In ZnO + C → Zn + CO, ZnO undergoes reduction.
Reason (R): Carbon is a reducing agent that reduces ZnO to Zn.
